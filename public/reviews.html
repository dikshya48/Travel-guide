France

Magical Experience in the City of Lights

France exceeded all my expectations! The Eiffel Tower at night is breathtaking, and the food is incredible. We stayed in a charming Airbnb in Le Marais which was perfect for exploring. The only downside was how crowded some attractions were, but booking tickets in advance helped.

Michael Chen

5.0
Japan

Absolutely Mind-Blowing Country

Japan is like nowhere else on Earth! The perfect blend of futuristic technology and ancient traditions. The food is incredible - from $10 ramen to $300 sushi, everything was amazing. Public transportation is so efficient it puts every other country to shame. Can't wait to go back!

Emma Rodriguez

Traveled in January 2023

3.0
Indonesia

Beautiful but Overcrowded

Indonesia's landscapes are stunning - the rice terraces and beaches are picture perfect. However, the islands have become extremely touristy. Ubud was packed with Instagram crowds, and the beaches were littered in some areas. Still enjoyed my time but would recommend visiting in shoulder season.
